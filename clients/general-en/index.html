About the Project

Documentary Film

Esther Wojcicki is a legendary teacher and journalist from Palo Alto, known as "The Godmother of Silicon Valley". Her educational philosophy TRICK (Trust, Respect, Independence, Collaboration, Kindness) has revolutionized approaches to teaching worldwide.

Her three daughters are tech icons: Susan Wojcicki — former CEO of YouTube, Anne Wojcicki — founder of 23andMe, and Janet Wojcicki — professor of epidemiology at UCSF. James Franco is involved in the production.

The documentary tells the story of a woman who raised three leaders of Silicon Valley and changed the way the world thinks about education.
